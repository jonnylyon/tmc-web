Private medical consulting rooms in a beautiful Victorian house

The Manchester Clinic provides modern medical consulting rooms in the beautiful leafy surroundings of the Ellesmere Park conservation area. With rooms available on both a sessional and occasional basis, The Manchester Clinic provides a range of services to consultants to suit their needs and the needs of their patients.

Less than 15 minutes′ drive from the city centre, The Manchester Clinic is well-connected with motorways, trains, trams & buses all within a short distance. If you would like to know more about The Manchester Clinic, please take a look at our services & prices, view photos of our beautiful house, or contact us for further information.
